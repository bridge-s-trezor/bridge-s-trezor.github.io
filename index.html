How to Get Started

Getting started with Trezor Bridge is simple. Follow these steps:

  1. Download Trezor Bridge from the official website.
  2. Install the application on your computer.
  3. Connect your Trezor hardware wallet.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

FAQs

What is Trezor Bridge?

Trezor Bridge is software that facilitates communication between your Trezor device and your computer's web browser.

How do I install Trezor Bridge?

To install Trezor Bridge, download the installer from the official Trezor website and follow the on-screen instructions.

Is Trezor Bridge secure?

Yes, Trezor Bridge is designed with advanced security protocols to ensure your cryptocurrencies are managed safely.
